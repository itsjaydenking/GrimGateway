Building Your Legend

Welcome to character creation in Light Omens. Here, every choice shapes your character’s journey and the legacy they leave behind. In the world of Light Omens, heroes, adventurers, and scholars often leave their mark on those who follow. Whether through Reminiscent Bonds with companions or Memorial Bonds with the fallen, your character’s story can inspire future generations. As you build your character, a seeker should consider the impact they hope to have and how their memory might guide others, even when they are no longer there to lead. You are not just creating a character for adventures but shaping a piece of the world’s history.

Attributes

A character’s attributes stand for their core abilities. They express how capable a character is when trying specific actions. Each attribute has a rating from 1 to 6, measuring a character's physical, mental, and social aspects.
